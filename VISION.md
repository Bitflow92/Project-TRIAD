# Project TRIAD Vision

> **Train smarter. Recover better. Race stronger.**

---

# Why Project TRIAD Exists

Project TRIAD exists to build the endurance coaching platform that I always wanted but could never find.

Today's athletes often rely on a collection of disconnected systems:

- training plans in one application
- gym programmes in another
- nutrition somewhere else
- recovery tracked separately
- coaching notes scattered across documents
- AI tools disconnected from training data

Project TRIAD aims to bring all of these elements together into a single, intelligent platform that supports every aspect of endurance performance.

The goal is not simply to log workouts.

The goal is to help athletes continuously improve through better decisions.

---

# The Philosophy

Project TRIAD is built on several core principles.

## Athlete First

Every design decision should improve the athlete's experience.

Technology should disappear into the background, allowing the athlete to focus entirely on training.

---

## Data Ownership

The athlete owns the data.

Training history, performance metrics and coaching information should never be locked inside a commercial platform.

Project TRIAD is self-hosted and designed to give complete control over personal data.

---

## Simplicity Before Complexity

Complex software often creates unnecessary friction.

Project TRIAD should always remain intuitive, even as powerful features are added.

The best interface is one that requires very little explanation.

---

## Conservative Coaching

Progress is built through consistency.

The platform should encourage long-term development rather than short-term performance gains.

Recommendations should reduce injury risk, support recovery and improve sustainable performance.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Small improvements, repeated consistently, produce extraordinary long-term results.

Every workout, every recovery session and every coaching decision should contribute towards becoming a stronger athlete.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Project TRIAD will evolve into a complete digital endurance coaching platform.

It will eventually integrate:

- Swimming
- Cycling
- Running
- Strength Training
- Mobility
- Recovery
- Nutrition
- Sleep
- Race Planning
- Performance Analytics
- Equipment Management
- Injury Prevention
- Artificial Intelligence
- Automation
- Wearable Devices

Rather than replacing a coach, Project TRIAD should become an intelligent coaching companion that supports both athletes and coaches.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a core part of Project TRIAD.

However, AI should never replace sound coaching principles.

Instead, AI should assist by:

- identifying trends
- detecting fatigue
- recommending progressions
- adapting training plans
- recognising injury risk
- explaining performance changes
- answering athlete questions

The athlete always remains in control.

---

# Technology Vision

Project TRIAD embraces modern software engineering principles.

The platform will remain:

- self-hosted
- open architecture
- modular
- version controlled
- containerised
- mobile first
- AI enabled

Every release should improve maintainability as well as functionality.

---

# Beyond Ironman

Although Project TRIAD begins with preparing for an Ironman, the vision extends much further.

The platform should eventually support:

- beginner athletes
- experienced age-group competitors
- elite endurance athletes
- coaches
- training groups
- multisport clubs

The architecture should always allow future expansion without requiring major redesign.

---

# Success

Project TRIAD will be considered successful when it becomes the first application opened before training and the last application reviewed after training.

It should become the athlete's trusted source of truth for every aspect of endurance performance.

---

# Our Guiding Question

Whenever a new feature is proposed, we ask one simple question:

> **Will this help the athlete train smarter, recover better or race stronger?**

If the answer is yes, it belongs in Project TRIAD.

If the answer is no, it should not be built.

---

# Our Motto

> **Every workout has a purpose. Every decision is informed. Every improvement is intentional.**

---

# The Journey

Project TRIAD is not a finished product.

It is a living platform that will evolve continuously through training, experimentation, technology and experience.

Every release represents another step towards building the ultimate digital endurance coaching companion.

The journey is as important as the destination.
