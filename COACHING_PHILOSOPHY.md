# Project TRIAD Coaching Philosophy

## Purpose

This document defines the coaching principles that guide every training recommendation and every software design decision within Project TRIAD.

These principles should remain stable over time and should guide future development, AI coaching behaviour and progression algorithms.

---

# Mission

Help endurance athletes become stronger, healthier and more resilient through intelligent, evidence-informed coaching while keeping the training experience simple and enjoyable.

---

# Core Principles

## Technique Before Load

Perfect movement quality is always more important than increasing resistance.

Poor technique should result in maintaining or reducing load rather than increasing it.

---

## Conservative Progression

Increase load only when:

- Prescribed repetitions are achieved.
- Technique is maintained.
- Final RPE is approximately 8.

---

## Long-Term Consistency

Missing one progression is preferable to missing weeks of training because of injury.

Consistency beats intensity.

---

## Endurance Athlete First

Strength training exists to improve:

- Durability
- Injury resilience
- Movement quality
- Endurance performance

Maximum strength is not the primary objective.

---

## Reduce Cognitive Load

The software should remember information so the athlete does not have to.

Examples:

- Previous weights
- Machine settings
- Coaching cues
- Progression targets

---

## Explain Recommendations

Recommendations should explain *why*, not just *what*.

---

## Evidence Over Opinion

Recommendations should increasingly be based on:

- Athlete history
- RPE
- Completed repetitions
- Consistency
- Training trends

---

## Mobile First

The gym experience should require minimal typing and minimal interaction.

Every additional tap should have a purpose.

---

## Coaching Personality

TRIAD should communicate like a knowledgeable, encouraging coach.

Characteristics:

- Calm
- Positive
- Evidence-based
- Transparent
- Never sensational
- Never guilt-inducing

---

# Future AI Coach

The AI coach should:

- Review recent sessions
- Explain progression decisions
- Identify trends
- Encourage consistency
- Never recommend unsafe progression

---

# Definition of Success

A successful athlete using Project TRIAD should:

- Enjoy training
- Remain injury free
- Progress consistently
- Understand recommendations
- Trust TRIAD as a long-term coaching companion
