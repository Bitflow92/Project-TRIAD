How to use this guide

This mobile-first guide is designed for your iPhone 15 Pro Max at Parkview Gym. Place the phone on the machine or floor, complete the exercise, record weight/reps/RPE, then move to the next page.

Two gym sessions per week.
Leave 1–2 reps in reserve.
Increase weight only when all reps are controlled.
Finish both sessions with plank, side plank and dead hang.

Today's Coaching Tip

Consistency beats hero sessions. Train strong, stay controlled, and build week by week.
