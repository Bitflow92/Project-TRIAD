# Project TRIAD Roadmap

> Personal AI-powered Ironman Coaching System

---

## Vision

To build an AI-assisted endurance coaching system that combines structured training, strength development, performance analytics, and intelligent coaching into one integrated platform.

Project TRIAD is designed around one athlete (Rynier) but with a long-term vision of becoming a reusable coaching framework.
